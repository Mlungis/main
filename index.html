How it works

Three steps from issue to resolved

No paperwork, no waiting in queues. Report it, track it, done.

Step 01

Submit a Report

Describe the issue, select a category, add your room number, and optionally attach photos. Takes under 2 minutes.

Step 02

Maintenance Reviews

The maintenance team sees your report instantly on their dashboard, prioritises it, and assigns it for action.

Step 03

Issue Resolved

Track your report status in real-time — Pending, In Progress, or Resolved. Get full visibility every step of the way.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built specifically for student residences — simple for residents, powerful for maintenance.

Photo Uploads

Attach up to 2 photos per report so maintenance knows exactly what to expect.

Real-Time Status

Reports update live — no refreshing, no guessing. Know exactly where your request stands.

Maintenance Dashboard

Full admin view with charts, filters, bulk actions, and inline status updates.

Secure & Role-Based

Residents and maintenance staff get separate, protected dashboards with Supabase Auth.

Auto-Cleanup

Resolved reports are automatically removed after 3 days to keep dashboards clean.

Category Filtering

Reports sorted by Plumbing, Electrical, Wi-Fi, Furniture, Cleaning and more.
